Our Team

Dascire team, took form around 2013 , when 2 college students decided to launch a startup for developing data products in a relatively new market, after a long path of research and gathering experience developing numerical algorithms and scalable applications, and a few data products for IRT and education, the company took form, We're still small, but by no means not capable, Leveraging the core member skills and our support member skills, Dascire is able to professionally deliver data driven products

Jose Luis Pisa , Chief Technology Officer

Systems and Computer engineer, Has worked in many IT companies over the years, including the World Health Organisation , Former member of the SICS research Group, Has IRT and Statistics Knowledge, Passionate by statistics has decided to pursue Graduate studies in statistics.

Sandra Guayambuco , Lead Scientist

Mathematician. Passionate by programming and education, attempts to leverage all her math knowledge to improve learning quality of her students, and develops tools to help teachers make math learning a significant experience, through creative lesson planning and cutting-edge technologies applied in education.

Juan Liberato , Chief Executive Officer

Systems and Computing Engineer. Senior developer at SICS Research Group, His fields of expertise encompass GIS, Financial Engineering and risk management, but specially Statistics and Item Response Theory, developer of open source IRT projects for Computer Adaptive Testing, attempts to mix theory with implementation getting state of the art research to real world applications
